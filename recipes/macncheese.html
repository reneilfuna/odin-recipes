Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Like mac and cheese? Hate the ready made or tinned options but are too lazy to cook? This recipe is for you!

In only 25 minutes, this easy stovetop recipe is a must try! Good news is that once you've made it a couple times, you won't be able to stop!

Ingredients

  • 1/8 ounce of elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups of grate cheddar cheese
  • ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to bite, around 8 minutes.
  2. At the same time, melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Add flour, salt, and pepper, and stir until smooth, around 5 minutes.
  4. Pour in milk slowly, while stirring continuously. Continue to cook and stir until mixture is smooth and bubbling, about 5 minutes, making sure the milk doesn't burn.
  5. Add cheddar cheese and stir until melted, around 2 to 4 minutes.
  6. Drain macaroni and fold into cheese sauce until coated.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
