Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Like mac and cheese? Hate the ready made or tinned options but are too lazy to cook? This recipe is for you!

In only 25 minutes, this easy stovetop recipe is a must try! Good news is that once you've made it a couple times, you won't be able to stop!

Ingredients

  • 1/8 ounce of elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups of grate cheddar cheese
  • ground black pepper to taste
