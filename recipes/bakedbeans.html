Baked Beans

Description

Bored of heinz baked beans?

If you have 15 minutes spare, why not try this delicious recipe and test your culinary skills!

Ingredients:

  • 2 teaspoons of rapeseed oil
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, peeled and finely diced
  • 1 stick of celery, finely diced
  • 1 tablespoon of red wine vinegar
  • 8 ounce can of chopped tomatoes
  • 1.5 teaspoons of light brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 400g can of haricot beans, drained and rinsed
  • 4 tablespoons of vegetable stock
  • freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat rapeseed oil in a non-stick saucepan.
  2. Add onion, carrot and celery; sauté over a medium heat for about 10 minutes or until softened.
  3. Carefully stir in vinegar; cook for 1 minute.
  4. Stir in tomatoes, sugar, paprika, Worcestershire sauce and black pepper.
  5. Bring to a simmer; cover and cook gently for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Stir in beans; add stock if sauce is looking a little thick. Bring back to a simmer; cover and cook gently for a further 8–10 minutes, until sauce is cooked and beans are hot.
  7. Serve baked beans along with egg, toast, etc - whatever you like!
