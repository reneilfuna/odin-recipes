Salmon Pitas

Description

This delicious and healthy snack is the perfect picnic treat!

Ingredients:

  • 55g of little gem lettuce, shredded
  • 1/4 cucumber, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon of low-fat greek yoghurt
  • 1 teaspoon of finely grated lemon zest
  • 140g of skinless salmon fillet, flaked
  • 4 round wholemeal pitta breads
  • ground black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In one bowl, combine lettuce and cucumber.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine mayo, yoghurt, chopped mint, lemon zest, and flaked salmon. Mix well and add black pepper to taste.
  3. Slit open pita breads to make 4 pockets.
  4. Stuff pita breads with salad and salmon mixture.
  5. Serve immediately or wrap and chill for later!
